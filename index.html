Why we're the best ?

Bonfleet provides a cashless fleet expense management app with pre-paid cards that drivers can use at any fuel & service station in India. Using the Bon Petro Card – fleet managers can get auditable expense reports on every vehicle & driver as well as have one common loyalty point system across all petrol pumps in India. Bonfleet customers include taxi owners, hotels/delivery chains, large car fleets, and logistics companies. Benefits for fleet managers include transactions being restricted to fleet expenses, route based restrictions, consolidated billing, flexible payment options, and software to improve accounting & fleet management.
